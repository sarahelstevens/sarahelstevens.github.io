Best Odds Fingerprint Login

Following the introduction of the Betslip on both the Best Odds and Punters Apps, a system was required to collect user credentials for each of the user's bookmaker accounts. By logging in to their bookmaker account, the connection would need to be established and kept active by the App with a visual indicator displaying the current status.

One time login

By allowing the user to login individually with any bookmaker at any time, an active connection can then be kept and maintained with Touch ID.

With a PIN fallback incorporated into the user flow, the solution has become an incredibly convenient product for price-sensitive customers.

An aggregated list of balances

For the duration of the active connection, users are able to see individual and combined balances of all their bookmaker accounts. By knowing how much is available to spend, the user can choose to bet with their favourite bookmakers accordingly and receive the best odds in the market they have chosen to bet on.
