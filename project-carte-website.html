Carte Crêpes Website

Carte Crêpes provides coffee and sweet treats to the staff and students of Melbourne University. Following hospitality and social media success, it was decided that the website needed a design upgrade prior to Semester 2, 2013.

A Melbourne University favourite among both students and staff alike

In collaboration with the client and the developer a custom 'menu' widget was developed and the content displayed with a series of personal ingredient icons. Combined with this, a blog was implemented as an SEO recommendation

and to amplify their overall content marketing strategy.A modern, single page layout was designed with a complementary mobile version for various mobile devices.
