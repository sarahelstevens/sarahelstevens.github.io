IT Masters Website

IT Masters are a unique provider of University Qualifications and Industry Certifications in both the IT and Project Management industries. In 2013 they required a complete desktop and mobile redesign including IA recommendations.

Bringing eligibility to the front

Subject structures and course outlines were communicated in a clear, tabulated layout so that potential students know all their options.

Every page funnels the user into the eligibility form to encourage them to sign up for the first stage of the marketing cycle.

Free short courses for all potential students

Part of the marketing strategy for IT Masters was to offer free short courses on topics closely related to the course offerings. Since launching the short courses and promoting them on site the sign ups have surpassed 150,000 students with Masters enrolments increasing consistently.
