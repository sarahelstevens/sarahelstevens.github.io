Punters iOS Form Guide

The Form category is the most highly visited section of each and every Punters product. Within the Form Guide, the Predictor tool attracts by far the largest amount of traffic and so in 2014 Punters required a complete redesign to make the popular desktop tool suitable for app and mobile web devices.

The Predictor tool, simplifying a data-rich experience

The brief was to deliver the full functionality of
the desktop product, whilst providing a platform that was simple to manipulate and also visually reminiscent of the desktop experience.

The result of each race is calculated based on a variety of contributing factors - each of which can be moved from 0 to 100, depending on how important they believe each criteria is to the outcome.

Leading the user to explore deeper

From 1.8 million sessions in 2015, the average user spent 12.05 minutes using our app, a clear indication of lasting engagement and the strategy to encourage users to learn and explore the deeper form data.
Every time the user loads the Punters App they are led straight into the upcoming races list for the day.
The Form Guide was placed front and centre to demonstrate how fast-moving Australian horse racing is as a sport.
