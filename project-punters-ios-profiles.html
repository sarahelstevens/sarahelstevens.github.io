Punters Profiles

Punters.com.au has an existing database of 70,000 members that are active contributors to the community in the areas of tipping, betting and communicating with other users. User profile pages are the most searched category on the website, as users want to monitor the tipping performance of their peers and decide whether or not to purchase their available tips for sale.

70,000 active members

Users can be followed - but so too can horses, jockeys and trainers be 'black booked' for later reference. Each category required a profile that felt visually aligned to the others and yet still distinctly different in terms of relevant metadata and purpose.

The approach for these profiles was to offer users a rich content menu of deeper stats and listed information. In order to do this, snippets of graphs or information are used and encourage users to click through for more.

Educating users step-by-step

Horse racing is flooded with historical race data that can be overwhelming to new users. By utilising this content menu approach, the most relevant information is promoted to the top of the profiles and immediately highlighted on the first page. The heavy statistical data can be accessed by exploring the deeper sections, but is initially hidden so as to slowly initiate the user.
